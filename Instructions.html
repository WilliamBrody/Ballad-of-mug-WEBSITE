Coming out of your hiding after 65 million years, you (Mug), the last remaining slugasaurous, finally takes your first breath of fresh air.

But suddenly, you are stuffed in a cage onto the back of an evil zookeeper’s truck, and before you know it, you are locked in a zoo.

Now it’s up to you to find the exit and escape. Will you be able to defeat the vicious animals in your path and escape the zoo?

Controller Move left and right by moving the joystick.
Press control __ to jump.
Press control __ to dash.
Press control __ to attack.
Hold control __ to climb.
Keyboard
Hold A and D to move left and right.
Press space to jump.
Press R to dash.
Hold W to climb.
Click the left mouse button to attack.
Analog
Move the mouse left and right to move.
Click the left mouse button to attack.

Avoid spikes and jump on platforms to reach the animal who guards the door to the next level.

Touching the spikes will automatically reset the level and push you back to the start of your level, losing some health.

Once you reach the animal, fight it until it loses all its health, unlocking the next level.

Defeat all animals to win the game and free yourself from the evil zookeeper.
